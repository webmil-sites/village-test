Portfolio Review

Show your portfolio to the experts: they will say what has to be done and what is already enough. You can choose the reviewer — in the registration form, specify who, first and foremost, you would like to receive feedback from. Be sure to sign up in advance, because the number of participants is limited, and the number of viewers is not: you can come to see what other participants have done, what kind of projects are presented and if these projects are relevant.

Conditions of participation and requirements for the portfolio you can find in the registration form below.
